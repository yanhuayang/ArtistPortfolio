Disparity

found material newspaper, watercolor, overlap, Nov, 2018, SJSU

The disparity between rich and poor are high along with developing the high technology. The rate of unemployment has been increased. Most low level labors would be replaced by machine. I experiened this at the first hand because every time I go back my country China from the US there some changes. Obviously, there are more technology than workers now in some industry. The developing process is too fast, so that many people have to be viped out from the model society. The last generation have been sacrifice for this country for several decades, but they have to accept the second elimination of fate become poorer. When I return from China to the US, the custom has been changed as well. The passport are scaned by the machine not human being anymore. Some people do not know about English have to wait for a long time to get help. High technology can change the way of how people greet to each other. They also change the way of living that few people can hold the big part of capital, but others have to become homelesses.
