Protective Talisman"福“

Casting, Bronze, Polished; Fall, 2018

The character pronounces "FU" means fortune and happiness. In China, peole write this calligraphy when Chinese luna new year is coming. They stick it on the door or windows upside down means "FU Dao". (Good fortune and happiness will come to my home.) Every Chinese will miss the home when they see this, and remind them to reunion. "福“ is missed a point when I made it intentionally because I thought there would not have a perfect life. The flower is winter rose. There is a saying "梅花香自苦寒来" (Winter is cold but the winter rose still blossoming) symbolizes that you should make effort before your achievement. When both flower and "FU" are conbined together means that good fortune and happiness comes out from equal efforts.
